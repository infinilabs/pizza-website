Pizza does things differently!

Pizza solves the challenge of managing massive data seamlessly. Imagine creating a collection and continuously adding documents, from zero to petabytes, without the need to worry about sharding or reindexing. Scaling your machine becomes effortless, ensuring a smooth, seamless, and painless experience for application developers.
Experience True Real-Time Search
Instantly see results as soon as data is inserted, no index refresh needed. Our system updates seamlessly in the background, providing immediate feedback and delivering the fastest search experience possible. Whether you’re indexing new content or querying existing data, our real-time capabilities ensure you stay ahead with accurate and up-to-the-second results.
Efficient In-Place Partial Updates
Say goodbye to pulling and pushing entire documents for minor changes. Our system allows you to update only the specific fields you need, directly in place, drastically reducing overhead and improving performance. Keep your data fresh and responsive with minimal effort.
Unified Search
Pizza offers advanced search for all data types, integrating numbers, geopoints, vectors, text, images, videos, and insights.
